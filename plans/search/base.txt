Para sa **Search Screen**, magandang laman ay naka‑focus sa mabilis na paghahanap at malinaw na resulta. Eto ang plan:

---

# 🔍 Search Screen Plan

### 🔺 Header
- **Search bar** (prominent sa taas, may magnifying glass icon).  
- **Voice search option** (optional).  
- **Filter icon** para sa advanced search.  

---

### 🔺 Tabs / Categories (slidable sa taas ng results)
- [All]  
- [Groups]  
- [Members]  
- [Posts]  
- [Events]  
- [Hashtags / Topics]  

---

### 🔺 Results Layout
- **All tab** → mixed results (group cards, member cards, posts).  
- **Groups tab** → group cards (profile picture, name, short description, member count, join/view button).  
- **Members tab** → member cards (profile picture, full name, username, hobbies, location, follow button).  
- **Posts tab** → post cards (author, timestamp, content, reactions).  
- **Events tab** → event cards (title, date/time, location, RSVP button).  
- **Hashtags tab** → trending topics, clickable to open related posts.  

---

### 🔺 Interaction
- **Tap result card** → open detail (group detail, member profile, post detail, event detail).  
- **Long press** → quick actions (follow, join, save, mute).  
- **Filters** → refine search (by type, date, popularity).  
- **Recent searches** → quick access sa history.  

---

### ✨ Extra Enhancements
- **Suggestions while typing** (autocomplete for groups, members, hashtags).  
- **Trending searches** section.  
- **Empty state**: “No results found” with illustration.  
- **Smart ranking**: prioritize groups/members na may connection sa user.  

---

👉 Sa ganitong setup, ang **Search Screen** ay magiging parang central discovery hub — madaling maghanap ng groups, members, posts, events, at topics sa iisang lugar.
