# 🎛 Dating Preferences Screen

### 🔺 Header
- **Title**: “Your Preferences”  
- **Save / Apply button** → para ma‑update agad ang feed.  

---

### 🔺 Core Filters
- **Age Range Slider** → min/max (e.g., 22–35).  
- **Preferred Gender** → Male / Female / Non‑binary / Any.  
- **Max Distance** → slider (e.g., 10 km, 50 km, 100 km).  

---

### 🔺 Personality & Compatibility
- **Match by MBTI** → toggle (on/off).  
- **Match by Love Language** → toggle.  
- **Match by Relationship Goal** → toggle.  

---

### 🔺 Lifestyle & Interests
- **Hobbies selector** → chips/tags (e.g., Hiking, Cooking, Gaming).  
- **Interests selector** → chips/tags (e.g., Tech, Art, Travel).  
- **Lifestyle tags** → e.g., “Pet lover”, “Environmentalist”.  

---

### 🔺 Privacy & Safety
- **Visibility toggle** → “Show me only to verified users” / “Hide my profile from certain groups.”  
- **Verification badge info** → encourage users to verify.  

---

### 🔺 Footer
- **Reset to Default** → clear all filters.  
- **Apply Preferences** → update Discovery feed.  

---

👉 In short:  
- **Core filters** = age, gender, distance.  
- **Compatibility toggles** = personality, love language, relationship goal.  
- **Lifestyle selectors** = hobbies, interests, lifestyle tags.  
- **Safety controls** = visibility, verification.
