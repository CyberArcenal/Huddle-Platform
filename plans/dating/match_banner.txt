Ang **Match Screen** ay yung special moment kapag nag‑mutual like ang dalawang users. Parang “celebration screen” siya na nagsisilbing transition mula sa Discovery/Profile papunta sa Chat.  

### 🔎 Magandang laman ng Match Screen

**Header / Overlay**
- Animated banner: *“It’s a Match!”*  
- Profile pictures ng dalawang users side‑by‑side (ikaw + siya).  
- Background effect (confetti, glow, heart animation).  

**Info Section**
- Names ng parehong users.  
- Short tagline: *“You both liked each other.”*  
- Optional compatibility meter (based on preferences/personality).  

**Actions**
- 💬 **Start Chat** → direct link to Messaging Screen.  
- 🔄 **Continue Browsing** → balik sa Discovery feed.  
- ⭐ **Send Super Like Gift** (optional gamification).  

**Footer / Safety**
- Reminder: *“Stay safe. Don’t share sensitive info too soon.”*  
- Quick access: Block/Report (kung kailangan).  

---

### ✨ Extra Enhancements
- **Mini bio preview** → short snippet ng match’s bio.  
- **Shared interests highlight** → “You both love Hiking and Jazz Music.”  
- **Event suggestion** → “Join the upcoming Singles Night in your area.”  

---

👉 In short:  
Ang **Match Screen** ay hindi profile page, kundi **celebration + gateway**. Pinapakita niya na may connection na, at binibigyan ka ng choice: magsimula ng chat o magpatuloy sa pag‑discover.
