# ❤️ Dating Discovery / Match Feed Screen

### 🔺 Header / Navigation
- **Title**: “Discover Matches”  
- **Filter icon** → open Dating Preferences (age, gender, distance).  
- **Search bar** (optional) → hanapin specific profiles.  

---

### 🔺 Match Cards (scroll/swipe)
- **Profile picture** (main image, tap to view gallery).  
- **Basic info**: name, age, location.  
- **Quick tags**: hobbies, interests, lifestyle tags.  
- **Personality snippet**: MBTI type, love language.  
- **Relationship goal badge**: Friendship, Dating, Long‑term, Marriage.  
- **Mutual preview**: shared groups/events.  
- **Action buttons**:  
  - ❤️ Like  
  - ❌ Pass  
  - ⭐ Super Like  

---

### 🔺 Interaction Flow
- **Swipe right** → Like.  
- **Swipe left** → Pass.  
- **Swipe up** → Super Like.  
- **Tap card** → open full Profile Detail Screen.  

---

### 🔺 Extra Enhancements
- **Dynamic feed** → auto‑refresh with new profiles.  
- **Highlight banner** → “You have 3 new matches today!”  
- **Empty state** → “No matches found. Adjust your preferences.”  
- **Safety badge** → verified users indicator.
