# 💬 Chat / Messaging Screen

### 🔺 Header
- **Match name + profile picture**  
- **Status indicator** → online/offline, last active.  
- **Safety tools** → Block, Report, Unmatch.  

---

### 🔺 Conversation Area
- **Message bubbles** (left/right alignment).  
- **Content types**:  
  - Text messages  
  - Emojis/stickers  
  - Media attachments (photos, short videos)  
  - Location share (optional)  
- **Read receipts** → checkmarks or “Seen”.  
- **Timestamp** per message.  

---

### 🔺 Input Bar
- **Text field** → type message.  
- **Attachment icon** → upload photo/video.  
- **Emoji/sticker button**.  
- **Send button** → prominent, easy to tap.  

---

### 🔺 Extra Enhancements
- **Conversation starter prompts** → “Ask about their hobbies” / “Share your favorite song.”  
- **Shared interests highlight** → pinned at top: “You both love Hiking and Jazz Music.”  
- **Event invite shortcut** → “Invite to Group Event.”  
- **Safety reminder** → subtle banner: “Don’t share sensitive info too soon.”  

---

👉 In short:  
- **Header** = identity + safety.  
- **Conversation area** = rich messaging with receipts.  
- **Input bar** = quick, intuitive sending.  
- **Extras** = prompts, shared interests, event invites.
