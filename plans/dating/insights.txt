# 📊 Dating Insights Screen

### 🔺 Header
- **Title**: “Your Dating Insights”  
- **Time filter** → Today / This Week / This Month / All Time  

---

### 🔺 Key Metrics (cards)
- **Total Matches** → bilang ng mutual likes.  
- **Messages Sent / Received** → activity level.  
- **Profile Views** → ilang beses nakita ang profile mo.  
- **Likes Received** → bilang ng users na nag‑like sa iyo.  
- **Super Likes Received** → bilang ng special likes.  

---

### 🔺 Compatibility Highlights
- **Top shared interests** → “Most of your matches love Hiking and Jazz Music.”  
- **Common love language** → “60% of your matches value Quality Time.”  
- **Relationship goal alignment** → “70% of matches also want Long‑term Relationship.”  

---

### 🔺 Trends & Graphs
- **Matches over time** → line chart (growth trend).  
- **Messages per day/week** → bar chart.  
- **Top hobbies that attract matches** → pie chart.  

---

### 🔺 Recommendations
- **Profile optimization tips** → “Add more photos to increase views.”  
- **Preference adjustment** → “Expand age range to find 15% more matches.”  
- **Engagement prompts** → “Send a message to your 3 newest matches.”  

---

### 🔺 Footer
- **Safety reminder** → “Stay safe. Report suspicious accounts.”  
- **CTA** → “Update Preferences” or “Boost Profile.”  

---

👉 In short:  
Ang **Dating Insights Screen** ay parang **analytics dashboard** na nag‑combine ng stats (matches, messages, likes), compatibility highlights (shared interests, love language, goals), at actionable recommendations.
