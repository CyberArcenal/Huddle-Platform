Para sa **Notification Screens**, magandang plan ay naka‑organize para madaling makita ng user ang mga updates at actions. Base sa schema mo, eto ang design:

---

# 🔔 Notification Screens Plan

### 🔺 Header
- **Title**: “Notifications”  
- **Tabs (optional)**: [All] [Unread] [Mentions] [System]  
- **Mark all as read** button  

---

### 🔺 Notification List (scrollable)
- **Notification card** per item:  
  - **Actor details**: profile picture, username, full name.  
  - **Message**: e.g., “John liked your post”, “Anna joined your group”.  
  - **Notification type**: like, comment, follow, join, system update.  
  - **Related model**: link to post, group, event, or user profile.  
  - **Time ago**: e.g., “5m ago”, “Yesterday”.  
  - **Read status**: bold highlight kung unread, normal text kung read.  

---

### 🔺 Interaction
- **Tap notification card** → open related content (post, group, event).  
- **Swipe left/right** → quick actions (mark as read, delete).  
- **Long press** → show more options (mute actor, notification settings).  

---

### 🔺 Extras
- **Grouped notifications**: “3 people liked your post” instead of multiple single likes.  
- **Actor preview**: kung multiple actors, show stacked profile pictures.  
- **Filter bar**: unread vs all.  
- **Empty state**: “No notifications yet” with illustration.  

---

### ✨ Extra Enhancements
- **Push notification settings**: toggle per type (likes, comments, follows, group invites).  
- **Highlight important**: system notifications (e.g., policy updates) pinned on top.  
- **Smart sorting**: newest first, but group similar actions.  

---

👉 Sa ganitong setup, ang **Notification Screen** ay magiging malinaw, interactive, at madaling i‑navigate.  

Gusto mo bang i‑expand ko rin ang **Notification Detail Screen** (yung dedicated view kapag pinindot ang isang notification, showing full context)?
