Sige, eto ang **recreated plan para sa Profile Settings Page** — naka‑ayos na parang wireframe hierarchy para malinaw ang flow:

---

# ⚙️ Profile Settings Page Plan

### 🔺 Header
- **Title**: “Profile Settings”  
- **Save / Apply button** → para ma‑update agad ang changes  

---

### 🔺 Account Information
- **Display Name** → may link papunta sa **Change Name Page** (with cooldown rules)  
- **Username** → read‑only (fixed identity)  
- **Email address** → verification status (verified / not verified)  
- **Phone number** → optional, for 2FA  
- **Password change** shortcut  

---

### 🔺 Privacy & Security
- **Profile visibility** → toggle (show in search / hide profile)  
- **Two‑Factor Authentication (2FA)** → enable/disable  
- **Active sessions list** → terminate sessions individually  
- **Blocked users list** → manage blocked accounts  

---

### 🔺 Notifications
- **Email notifications** → toggle (likes, matches, messages)  
- **Push notifications** → toggle (app alerts)  
- **Digest frequency** → daily / weekly summary  

---

### 🔺 Preferences
- **Dating preferences shortcut** → age range, distance, gender  
- **Content preferences** → “Show me events”, “Suggest date ideas”  
- **Language & Region** → localization settings  

---

### 🔺 Account Management
- **Deactivate account** → temporary disable  
- **Delete account** → permanent removal  
- **Export data** → download profile & activity history  

---

👉 In short:  
- **Profile page** = identity visuals (profile pic, cover photo, bio).  
- **Settings page** = control center (name change, privacy, security, notifications, preferences, account management).
