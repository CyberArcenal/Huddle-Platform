# 📱 Group Detail Screen Plan

### 🔺 Header Section
- **Group profile picture**  
- **Group name** (bold, prominent)  
- **Member count** (formatted, e.g. “1.2k members”)  
- **Group type** (Hobby, Work, School, Cause)  
- **Short description** (1–2 lines)  
- **Long description** (expandable text)  
- **Membership status** (`is_member`)  
- **Action buttons**:  
  - *Join / Leave* (dynamic depende sa status)  
  - *Invite Friends*  

---

### 🔺 Tabs (slidable sa taas ng content)
- **Posts / Feed**  
- **Events**  
- **Members**  
- **About**  

---

### 📋 Tab Contents

#### 1. Posts / Feed Tab
- **Create Post button**  
  - Option A: *Top composer* (“What’s on your mind?” style, with profile pic + text input).  
  - Option B: *Floating Action Button (FAB)* sa lower‑right corner, “+” icon → options (Text, Photo/Video, Poll).  
- **Post cards**:  
  - Author info (profile pic, name, role).  
  - Timestamp.  
  - Content (text, image, video).  
  - Reactions + comments.  
- **Feed layout**: scrollable, newest posts on top.

#### 2. Events Tab
- Upcoming events list (title, date/time, location).  
- RSVP status (Going, Interested, Not Going).  
- Event capacity + attendees preview.  
- *Create Event* button (kung admin).  

#### 3. Members Tab
- Member list with:  
  - Profile picture, full name, hobbies/personality type.  
  - Role badge (Admin, Moderator, Member).  
  - Joined date.  
- Search bar para maghanap ng member.  
- Role management (kung admin).  

#### 4. About Tab
- Full group description.  
- Group rules / guidelines.  
- Group creation date.  
- Admin contact info.  

---

### ✨ Extra Enhancements
- Sticky header (group name + join/leave button always visible).  
- Animated transitions kapag nag‑switch ng tab.  
- Invite friends shortcut.  
- Contextual CTA (Create Post button hidden kung hindi pa member).  

---

👉 Sa ganitong plan, malinaw ang hierarchy: **Header → Tabs → Content per tab**, at may **Create Post button** sa Posts tab para maging interactive ang group.
