Para sa **Group List / Discovery Screen**, magandang laman ay yung mga impormasyon na makakatulong sa user na mabilis makilala at makapili ng group. Base sa schema mo, eto ang recommended content:

### 📋 Laman ng Group List / Discovery Screen
- **Group Card (per group)**  
  - **Profile picture** → maliit na icon/logo ng group.  
  - **Group name** → malinaw at naka‑bold.  
  - **Short description** → 1–2 linya para sa context.  
  - **Member count** → formatted (hal. “1.2k members”).  
  - **Group type** → hobby, school, work, cause (may badge o label).  
  - **Membership status** → indicator kung *joined* na o hindi.  

- **List layout**  
  - Scrollable feed ng group cards.  
  - Search bar sa taas para maghanap ng group.  
  - Filter chips (hal. “Hobbies”, “Work”, “School”, “Causes”).  
  - Sorting options (hal. “Most popular”, “Newest”, “Nearby”).  

- **Member preview (optional)**  
  - Sa bawat group card, puwede magpakita ng 2–3 profile pictures ng active members.  
  - Nagbibigay ng sense ng community at activity level.  

- **Call to action**  
  - Button: *Join* o *View Group*.  
  - Kung hindi pa member, makikita ang *Join*; kung member na, makikita ang *View*.  

---

### ✨ Extra Enhancements
- **Recommendation section**: “Groups you may like” based on hobbies/interests ng user.  
- **Trending groups**: highlight ng mga groups na mabilis lumalaki.  
- **Nearby groups**: kung may location data, puwede mag‑suggest ng local communities.  

---

👉 Sa ganitong design, ang **Group List / Discovery Screen** ay magiging parang marketplace ng communities — mabilis makita ang identity ng group, activity level, at kung relevant siya sa user.
