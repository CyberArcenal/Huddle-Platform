Maganda yang idea mo — parang **stories style filter bar** sa taas ng Group Feed. Eto ang plan:

---

# 📰 Group Feed / Home Screen with Group Circles

### 🔺 Top Filter Bar (slidable circles)
- **First circle = “All Groups”** → lahat ng posts mula sa groups na kasali ang user.  
- **Next circles = bawat group** → profile picture ng group (bilog), clickable.  
- **Horizontal scroll** → puwedeng i‑slide para makita lahat ng groups.  
- **Active state** → naka‑highlight ang kasalukuyang filter (hal. may border o glow).  

---

### 🔺 Feed Content (depends on selected circle)
- **All Groups** → aggregated feed ng lahat ng groups.  
- **Specific Group** → feed ng posts mula sa napiling group lang.  

---

### 🔺 Post Composer
- Visible kung member ng group.  
- “What’s on your mind?” field + media upload + poll option.  
- Post button.  

---

### 🔺 Post Cards
- Author info (profile pic, name, role).  
- Timestamp.  
- Content (text, image, video).  
- Reactions + comments.  
- Share button.  

---

### ✨ Extra Enhancements
- **Pinned posts**: announcements sa taas ng feed.  
- **Upcoming events preview**: kung group filter ay active.  
- **Smart sorting**: newest, most popular, media only.  
- **FAB (Floating Action Button)**: quick create post kahit anong filter.  

---

👉 Sa ganitong design, parang **Instagram stories bar** pero pang‑filter ng groups. User makakakita agad ng “All Groups” feed, tapos puwede mag‑slide para pumili ng specific group feed.
