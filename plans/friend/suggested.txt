### Overview
Gusto kong gawing **Suggested Friends** bilang isang contextual, explainable discovery feed — hindi random list kundi mga rekomendasyong may dahilan. Target: mabilis na koneksyon na may konteksto (bakit sila inirerekomenda), madaling aksyon (Add Friend, Preview, Hide), at kontrol ng user.

---

### Pinakamahalagang attributes (priority table)
| **Attribute** | **Why it matters** | **Client label** |
|---|---:|---|
| **Mutuals** | Pinakamalakas na social signal | **3 mutuals** |
| **Shared interests** | Mas mataas conversion kapag may common hobbies | **Same interest: Photography** |
| **Recent interaction** | Profile views / likes increase relevance | **Viewed you 2d ago** |
| **Proximity** | Useful kung app supports location | **Nearby 1.2 km** |
| **Recency** | New users benefit from onboarding boosts | **Joined 5 days ago** |

---

### UI layout and components
- **Header**: title **Suggested**, small filter icon, lightweight sort (Mutuals / Interests / Nearby).  
- **Filter chips**: Mutuals; Interests; Nearby; New.  
- **Card** per suggestion: avatar; **name** bold; **reason chip** (e.g., “3 mutuals”); one‑line bio or shared interest; primary **Add Friend** button; secondary **Preview** (opens bottom sheet); overflow menu **Hide** / **Report**.  
- **Compact grid option**: 2‑column cards for quick browsing; toggle in header.  
- **Empty state**: CTA **Find people** or **Sync contacts**.  
- **Microcopy**: show **score** or short explanation under name when space allows: “Suggested because you both follow Ana and like Hiking.”

---

### Ranking, scoring, and backend expectations
- **Scoring model**: weighted sum (example weights: Mutuals 40, Shared interests 25, Interaction 15, Proximity 10, Recency 10).  
- **Diversity**: add small randomness and category diversity to avoid echo chamber.  
- **Explainability**: return `reason` and `score` in API so client can surface “Because…” text.  
- **API contract**: `GET /friend-suggestions/?filter=&page=` returns `[{id,name,avatar,mutual_count,shared_interests,reason,score,distance_km}]`.  
- **Client behavior**: sort client‑side by score, allow filter chips to request backend when needed.

---

### Interactions and microflows
- **Add Friend**: tap → button disables + spinner → optimistic show **Pending**; call `POST /friends/requests/send/ { to_user_id }`; on failure rollback and show toast. Show Undo snackbar for a few seconds.  
- **Preview**: bottom sheet with mutuals, recent posts, and actions (Message, Add Friend, View Profile).  
- **Hide / Report**: overflow menu; hide reduces similar future suggestions; report opens moderation flow.  
- **Filters**: quick client filters for shared interests; complex filters call backend.  
- **A/B testing**: experiment with ordering (mutuals-first vs. interests-first) to maximize accept rate.

---

### Metrics, privacy, and safety
- **Metrics to track**: suggestion click rate, add friend conversion, undo rate, hide/report rate, time to add.  
- **Privacy**: respect user opt‑outs and blocked lists; do not surface location unless user consented.  
- **Abuse control**: throttle suggestion frequency and Add Friend actions to prevent spam; surface rate‑limit messages gracefully.  
- **Quality loop**: use conversion signals to reweight scoring (learn which signals predict acceptance).
