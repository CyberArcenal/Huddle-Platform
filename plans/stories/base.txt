Para sa **Stories feature**, maganda kung may ilang complementary screens para kumpleto ang experience ng user. Hindi lang siya isang viewer, kundi may ecosystem ng **creation, viewing, highlights, at analytics**. Eto ang detailed plan:

---

# 📖 Stories Screens Plan

### 🔺 1. Stories Feed / Viewer Screen
- **Top bar**: horizontal scroll ng story circles (profile pic ng user/group).  
- **Tap circle** → open story viewer.  
- **Story viewer**:  
  - Full‑screen media (photo/video).  
  - Overlay: username, timestamp, reactions.  
  - Navigation: tap right → next story, tap left → previous, swipe down → exit.  
  - Quick actions: react, comment (bottomsheet), share.  

---

### 🔺 2. Create Story Screen
- **Composer**:  
  - Capture photo/video (camera integration).  
  - Upload from gallery.  
  - Add text overlay, stickers, emojis.  
  - Apply filters/themes.  
- **Privacy selector**: Public, Followers, Group members.  
- **Post button** → publish story.  

---

### 🔺 3. Story Highlights Screen
- **Highlights section** sa profile.  
- User can **save stories** into highlight collections.  
- Each highlight = circle with cover image + title.  
- Tap highlight → viewer of saved stories.  

---

### 🔺 4. Story Detail / Analytics Screen (for author)
- **Stats**:  
  - View count.  
  - Reactions breakdown.  
  - Who viewed (list of users).  
- **Actions**:  
  - Delete story.  
  - Add to highlights.  
  - Share story link.  

---

### 🔺 5. Group Stories Screen (optional)
- Dedicated tab sa group profile.  
- Shows stories posted by group members.  
- Same viewer flow, but scoped to group context.  

---

### ✨ Extra Enhancements
- **Auto‑play**: continuous playback across stories.  
- **Mute/unmute** toggle.  
- **Progress bar** per story.  
- **Pinned story** option (admin).  
- **Cross‑posting**: share story to multiple groups.  

---

👉 Sa ganitong setup, may **5 key screens**:  
1. Stories Feed/Viewer  
2. Create Story  
3. Story Highlights  
4. Story Detail/Analytics  
5. Group Stories (optional)  

Parang Instagram/Facebook style pero mas modular at aligned sa ecosystem ng groups, posts, at bookmarks na na‑design na natin.
