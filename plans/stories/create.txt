Para sa **Create Story Screen**, maganda kung simple pero flexible ang laman para makapag‑express ang user ng mabilis. Eto ang detailed breakdown:

---

# 📸 Create Story Screen

### 🔺 Header
- **Title**: “Create Story”  
- **Close button (X)** → exit composer  
- **Post button** → publish story  

---

### 🔺 Media Capture / Upload
- **Camera integration** → take photo or record video.  
- **Gallery upload** → pick existing photo/video.  
- **Preview area** → full‑screen preview ng media bago i‑post.  

---

### 🔺 Editing Tools
- **Text overlay** → add captions, quotes, hashtags.  
- **Stickers & emojis** → fun elements.  
- **Drawing tools** → freehand doodles.  
- **Filters** → color grading, effects.  
- **Resize & crop** → adjust framing.  

---

### 🔺 Enhancements
- **Music overlay** → add background track.  
- **Location tag** → optional geotag.  
- **Tag users** → mention friends.  
- **Poll / Q&A sticker** → interactive story element.  

---

### 🔺 Privacy & Settings
- **Audience selector**: Public, Followers, Group members.  
- **Expiry**: default 24h, option to save to Highlights.  
- **Allow replies** toggle (on/off).  

---

### 🔺 Preview & Publish
- **Preview mode** → see full story before posting.  
- **Publish button** → post story live.  
- **Save to device** option → keep a copy locally.  

---

👉 Sa ganitong setup, ang **Create Story Screen** ay parang mini‑studio: mabilis mag‑capture, may editing tools, interactive stickers, privacy controls, at preview bago i‑publish. Familiar flow (Instagram/Facebook style) pero puwede mong i‑expand para sa **Groups** at **Highlights** integration.
